John Deacon

Date Of Birth August 19, 1951
Birthplace Leicester, England
Instruments Bass guitar, double bass, keyboards

John Deacon’s first guitar was a red plastic Tommy Steele special, which his parents bought for him when he was seven.

John’s father, Arthur, however, led him in a different direction, encouraging John to tinker with electronics, a hobby that Arthur himself loved. One of John's early projects included adapting an old reel-to-reel tape deck to record music from the radio - usually The Beatles and Alan Freeman's Hit Parade.

John’s interest in electronics developed into a passion, and in his younger years at least, he thought of turning his hobby into a career.
